The mark

A serif italic wordmark paired with a precision coordinate mark — a quiet nod to engineering and exactness, without literal industrial clichés.

◇ Do

  • Keep clear space ≥ the height of the “N” around the lockup.
  • Use coral only for the mark or a single accent detail.
  • Place on cream, paper, or ink backgrounds.

✕ Don't

  • Recolor the wordmark or stretch / condense it.
  • Add shadows, outlines, or gradients to the mark.
  • Place on busy photos without a scrim.

05 — UsageVoice & motifs

Voice & motifs

Tone of voice

Confident, clear, and warm. Strategic without jargon. Speak in outcomes and systems — “repeatable,” “aligned,” “measurable.” Bilingual ES / EN, never mixed mid-sentence.

Graphic motifs

The coordinate mark, a fine registration crosshair, hairline corner ticks, and a faint coordinate grid. Used quietly — structure, not decoration.
